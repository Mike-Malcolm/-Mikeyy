Chapati

Chapati, also known as roti,rooti,rotee rotii,rotta,safati,shabati, phulka,chapo,sada roti,poli and roshi, is an unlevened flatbread originating from the Indian subcontinent and is a stple in India, Nepal, Bangldesh,Pakistan,Sri Lanka,East Africa, the Arabican Peninsula and the Caribbean.

INGREDIENTS

  • Flour
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Water

PREPARATIONS

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients.
  2. Add lukewarm water
  3. Knead the dough
  4. Add cooking oil
  5. Allow to rest
  6. Ready to cook
